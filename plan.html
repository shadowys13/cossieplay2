Goals

The project is that set a website for a small family-run local business which runs on custom cosplay, selling costume dress and a monthly cosplay party. The owners of this small business want to improve their business through Internet to increase the number customers and appeal more young audience to join in cosplay parties, especially high school kids and young adults.

Success Evaluation

This website helps Cossieplay to attract many new customers, which in age group between 18 to 30. It gets more than 100 people to join in monthly parties and makes 20 customers at least per week. This business also sells and rents many costumes for big Cosplay groups.

Target Audience

The target audience of this site is younger generation which includes students and adults who loves cosplay. The website is not serious and boring. It combines some special colors and attracts the customers' attentions. People will feel relaxed and eased when they are searching this website.
